Privacy Policy

App: Recipe Receipt
Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Recipe Receipt ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address and password (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name / username (chosen by you)
  • Profile photo (optionally uploaded by you)
  • Recipes and ingredients you create
  • Shopping list items you add
  • Device locale and language preferences
  • App settings (for example, theme and currency)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Store and sync your recipes and shopping list across devices
  • Display your profile within the app
  • Provide ingredient and recipe language features (including ML-based features where applicable)
  • Provide premium features and process in-app purchases
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) for account creation and login
  • Cloud Firestore (Google) for cloud storage of your app data
  • Google Sign-In for optional account login
  • Google ML Kit for language/translation-related features
  • Google Play Billing for in-app purchase processing

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from within the app. Account deletion permanently removes your data (including recipes, shopping list, and profile information) from our systems.

6. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Access and update your profile data in the app
  • Change your password
  • Request a password reset by email
  • Delete your account and associated data in-app

7. Data Security

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • Authentication and storage rely on Google Firebase security infrastructure
  • We do not sell your personal data

8. Children's Privacy

Recipe Receipt is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
